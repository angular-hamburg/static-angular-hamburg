What is it all about?

The angular.hamburg meetup is a meetup around the Angular framework which takes place in Hamburg. We're open to anyone interested in the topic. Whether you're a beginner dying to poke questions at someone with experience or a veteran wanting to discuss the finer details, everyone is welcome.

When do we all meet again?

In thunder, lightning, or in rain? When the hurlyburly's done. When the battles lost and won.
